This Privacy Policy explains how CozyPup collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using CozyPup, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account info: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email, name, and avatar (Google only).
  • Pet profiles: Names, species, breed, birthday, weight, and avatars you create.
  • Chat history: Your conversations with the AI assistant.
  • Calendar and reminders: Events, reminders, and related notes you create.
  • Images you upload: User and pet avatars, plus photos attached in chat.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device info: Model, iOS version, app version (used for diagnostics and compatibility).
  • Usage logs: API request records with anonymized request IDs for troubleshooting and rate limiting.
  • Push tokens: If you enable notifications, we store your APNs token to deliver reminders.
  • Location (only when you use "Find nearby vet"): We fetch your current location once and send it to Google Places API. No background tracking.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core features (AI chat, pet management, calendar, reminders, emergency guidance).
  • Send conversation content to third-party LLM providers to generate responses.
  • Prevent abuse (rate limiting, error tracking).
  • Interact with Apple App Store for subscription billing matters.
  • Improve the product via aggregated, anonymized analytics (no personal identifiers).

3. Third-Party Services

CozyPup relies on the following third parties:

  • Google Cloud Platform (database, file storage, deployment) — Montreal, Canada data center.
  • LiteLLM / DeepSeek / Moonshot / OpenAI (LLM inference and embeddings) — Conversation content is sent to these providers to generate responses. Providers contractually agree not to use the data for model training.
  • Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In (authentication).
  • Google Places API (nearby vet search).
  • Apple App Store (subscription billing).
  • Apple Push Notification Service (push notifications).

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Google Cloud in Montreal, Canada.
  • Images are stored in Google Cloud Storage.
  • Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • We take reasonable technical measures to prevent unauthorized access, but no online service can be 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share data with third parties in the following cases:

  • The third-party services listed above, only as needed for functionality.
  • When required by law (e.g., court order).
  • With your explicit consent.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data via the Profile page and chat history.
  • Deletion: Tap "Delete Account" at the bottom of the Profile page to permanently delete all your data. This cannot be undone.
  • Correction: You can edit pet profiles, name, avatar, etc. at any time.
  • Export: For structured data export, contact us.

7. Children's Privacy

CozyPup is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover your child has used CozyPup, please contact us to have the data removed.

8. International Data Transfer

If you use CozyPup outside Canada, your data may be transferred to other countries (e.g., the United States for LLM inference). These transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via in-app notification or email. Continued use of CozyPup means you accept the updated policy.
