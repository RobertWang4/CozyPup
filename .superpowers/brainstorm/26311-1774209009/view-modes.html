Navigation Flow

Calendar ──tab switch──▶ Multi-day Timeline
Calendar ──double tap──▶ Single-day Timeline
Multi-day ──tap a day───▶ Single-day Timeline
Single-day ──← back─────▶ Multi-day or Calendar
